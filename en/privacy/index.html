Privacy policy

Technical data, local search, external links and visitor rights.

No account or form

This version of the website provides no user account, contact form or proprietary newsletter subscription. The website does not automatically collect email addresses.

Local search

Internal search runs in your browser. Search terms are not sent to the editor or to a third-party search service.

Audience measurement with Plausible

The website uses Plausible Analytics to measure page views, traffic sources and, for selected links, clicks to the newsletter. Plausible may produce aggregate statistics about country or region, browser, operating system and device type. The purpose is to understand overall use and improve content, without advertising or individual profiling.

Plausible uses no cookies, local storage or persistent identifier. The IP address and user agent are used transiently to create a daily identifier; they are not stored and the calculation salt is deleted every 24 hours. Visitor data is processed in the European Union. Read Plausible’s data policy ↗

Analytics retention

Retention depends on the Plausible plan in use: the provider currently lists three years for Starter and Growth, five years for Business, and a contractual period for Enterprise. The editor controls the account and may delete the site statistics at any time. This information is checked whenever this policy is revised.

Local questionnaire data

The assessment tools run locally and may retain up to 50 results in your browser to show an aggregate local history. No identifying data is requested and no collective transmission is active while no collection endpoint is configured. You can erase these records by clearing this website’s local data in your browser settings.

Technical hosting

The website may be hosted by GitHub Pages. Like any web host, GitHub may process technical data required to secure and deliver the service, including connection logs, under its own privacy policy.

External links and services

Opening a publication, LinkedIn, Substack or another external website is governed by that service’s privacy policy. Before any visitor action, the browser currently loads Google Fonts and the Plausible measurement script described above.

Contact and data minimisation

Contact is voluntary through LinkedIn. Do not send health data, confidential company information or sensitive personal data.

Your rights

Where information is sent directly to the editor, you may request access, correction or deletion. You may also contact the French data protection authority, CNIL.

Update

Last revised: 19 July 2026. This page will be updated before any change to forms, collection, retention periods or service providers.

Privacy policy

Personal data, newsletter registration and external services.

Newsletter registration

The form collects the email address and associated consent. Once the double-confirmation service is activated, an address is added only after the user validates the confirmation link. Until then, the form opens a structured request in the user’s email client and the website does not automatically transmit the address.

Legal basis and retention

Processing is based on consent. Confirmed addresses are retained until consent is withdrawn or the user unsubscribes. Each newsletter should provide a simple unsubscribe method.

Recipients

Data are accessible to the site editor and, once configured, the technical provider strictly necessary for confirmation and delivery. The provider’s name and any relevant international transfers must be added to the French policy when activated.

Cookies and analytics

This version does not include advertising cookies or a third-party audience-measurement script. Local search runs in the browser and does not send search terms to an external service.

External links

Opening an external source, LinkedIn or an email client is governed by that service’s privacy policy.

Your rights

You may request access, correction or deletion, withdraw consent and object to future messages by writing to santetravailia@gmail.com. You may also contact the French data protection authority, CNIL.

Security

Do not submit health data, confidential company information or sensitive personal data through the newsletter or contact links.

Reference version

The French privacy policy is the reference version for detailed compliance information.

Scope

A work-centred reading of artificial intelligence

The site distinguishes three realities that should not be conflated: generative AI and language models; predictive or decision-support systems; and algorithmic management, monitoring and automated work allocation.

Each topic is examined through real tasks, data flows, decision rights, verification time, professional responsibility, collective work and occupational health.

Editorial method

Source, date and distinguish

A legal obligation, a scientific result, a first-instance ruling and a professional interpretation do not have the same evidential status. The site makes these differences visible and states uncertainty when evidence remains limited.
